Txti has retired

Txti was created in 2014 with a simple and benevolent mission:to play a part in a more
globally accessible internet.Our slogen was "fast wab pages for everybody," and it
worked! Txti served millions of people, with well over half a million fast web pages created.
since then, the whole world has change. Now, there is less need for web pages that
work on flip phones, and more need for trustworthiness and safety on the internet.As txti
is not able to keep up with this need,it's time for it to step aside to ensure we're not
causing harm.

Thank you to everybody who used and loved txti.lt was a fun and useful project
that l simple wasn't able to maintain. there are many lessons learned here, but i am grateful to
have been part of a special era of the internet's evolution.
